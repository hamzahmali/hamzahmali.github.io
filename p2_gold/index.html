Dash Knight

By Hamzah Ali

EECS 494 – P2 Gold

Dash Knight is a top-down action game built in Unity. The core mechanic is a directional dash that can phase through enemies and hazards, while also enabling aggressive movement-based combat. The game emphasizes fast decisions, clean feedback, and tight control.

▶ Play Online (itch.io)

Core Features

Directional Phase Dash

A fast dash triggered with Shift + a direction. During the dash, the player can phase through enemies and certain obstacles, while a dash hitbox activates to deal damage to enemies passed through. Outer walls block the dash, requiring intentional positioning and timing.

Combat Feedback: Hit Flash + Particle Effects

To make combat feel responsive and satisfying, I implemented hit flash and particle effects when enemies take damage. These visual cues reinforce impact and clearly communicate successful attacks.

Enemy AI + Animator-Driven Attacks

Enemies chase the player using Rigidbody-based movement and attack using animation-driven hitboxes. Animation events toggle attack colliders on/off so damage timing matches the visual swing.

Game Loop and Restart

The game supports a complete loop: start from the main menu, play through the level, and return/restart cleanly without needing to stop the Unity process.
